## Idea 1: TestimonialCapture

**Category:** B2B SaaS

**Problem:** Small businesses struggle to collect and showcase customer testimonials, losing trust and conversions.

**Solution:** Automated testimonial collection platform that sends timed email requests to customers, captures video/text testimonials, and displays them on beautiful embeddable widgets.

**Target Customer:** Solo founders and small B2B SaaS companies (1-10 employees) who lack testimonials on their landing pages.

**Revenue Model:** Subscription - $29/mo for up to 100 testimonials, $79/mo for unlimited

**Time to Launch:** 4 weeks
