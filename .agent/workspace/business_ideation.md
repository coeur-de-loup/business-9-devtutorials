### Idea 1: ContractGuard AI
**One-line description:** AI-powered contract analysis and risk assessment for small businesses
**Target customer:** Small business owners and solo entrepreneurs reviewing contracts
**Problem:** Spending hours reviewing contracts manually, missing hidden risks, and understanding legal terms
**Solution:** Upload contracts and get AI-powered analysis of key terms, risks, recommendations, and plain-English explanations
**Pricing:** $29/month per user, $99/month for advanced features
**Why simple:** Uses existing AI APIs (OpenAI), simple file upload interface, templated analysis
**Why $1k/mo:** Pain is immediate and costly, clear value prop, low barrier to try
