## Tips for Success

1. **Thread length:** Keep threads to 10-15 tweets max
2. **Visuals:** Use images/charts in 50% of tweets
3. **Consistency:** Post daily, even if engagement is low at first
4. **Engagement:** Reply to every comment within 1 hour
5. **Authenticity:** Be yourself, don't sound like a corporate account
6. **Value first:** 70% of tweets should provide value, not promote
7. **Hashtags:** Use 2-3 max per tweet (#webdev #javascript #react)
8. **Tagging:** Tag creators (with permission) to amplify reach
9. **Threads:** Turn popular tweets into threads for more depth
10. **Analytics:** Review weekly, double down on what works
