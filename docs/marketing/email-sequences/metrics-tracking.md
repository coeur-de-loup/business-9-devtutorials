## Tools & Setup

### Recommended Email Service Providers

**For MVP (Free Tier):**
1. **Mailchimp** - Free up to 500 subscribers, good automation
2. **SendGrid** - Free up to 100 emails/day, reliable delivery
3. **Brevo (Sendinblue)** - Free up to 300 emails/day, good analytics

**For Growth (Paid):**
1. **ConvertKit** - Creator-focused, powerful segmentation
2. **ActiveCampaign** - Advanced automation, behavioral targeting
3. **Postmark** - Excellent deliverability for transactional emails
