**Test Variables:**
1. Subject lines (5 options per email)

3. Call-to-action placement
4. Discount amount (20% vs 30% vs 40%)
5. Email timing (day of week, time of day)

**Testing Tools:**
- ConvertKit or EmailOctopus (free tier)
- Track opens, clicks, conversions
- Minimum sample size: 500 emails per variant

**Success Criteria:**
- Statistically significant winner (p < 0.05)
- Implement winner for future sequences
