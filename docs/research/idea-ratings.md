## Rating Criteria

Each idea is rated on:
1. **Simplicity** (1-5): Can 1 person build in 4-6 weeks?
2. **Profitability** (1-5): Clear path to $1,000/month?
3. **Market Pain** (1-5): How urgent is the problem?
4. **Competition Gap** (1-5): Is there an underserved segment?
5. **Free/Local Dev** (1-5): Can it be built without spending?

**Total Score:** /25
**Minimum to proceed:** 15/20

1. **Highest Pain Score (5/5)**

   - One bad contract can bankrupt a freelancer

2. **No Competition in Segment (5/5)**
   - LegalZoom: $10-20/document (not review)

   - **Gap:** No simple freelancer contract review tools under $50/mo

3. **Exploding Market (5/5)**
   - Legal tech: $31.59B → $63.59B by 2032 (9.4% CAGR)
   - AI in legal: $1.9B growing at 13.1% CAGR
   - Freelance economy growing (gig workforce)

4. **Simple Build (4/5)**
   - Rule-based NLP (spaCy is free)
   - PDF parsing (PyPDF is free)
   - 20 common risky clauses library
   - GPT-4o-mini for suggestions
   - No complex ML needed

5. **Clear ROI (5/5)**
   - 300 customers × $15/mo = $4,500/mo
   - Prevents one bad contract = pays for 10+ years
   - Cheaper than 1 hour of lawyer time

6. **Free/Local Development (5/5)**
   - All tools have free tiers
   - No paid APIs required for MVP
   - Can build and test locally
